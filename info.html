6 Important Points about Goldendoodles

  1. Goldendoodles are the perfect mix.
    • They are the adorable product of a godlen retriever and a standard poodle.
  2. Goldendoodles are great for those with allergies.
    • Because goldendoodles have a poodle parent, many are hypoallergenic. They shed minimally which is great for those with allergies and people who don't want to have to constantly vacuum.
  3. Goldendoodles are incredibly athletic.
    • They love to swim and will quickly become your new running buddy!
  4. Goldendoodles are very friendly.
    • This makes them the perfect pet for any family partiuclarly with young children.They are gentle and make great playmates.
  5. Goldendoodles come in various sizes.
    • For those who don't want a big dog, a minature Goldendoodle may be perfect. Instead of being bred with a Standard Poodle, Golden Retrievers are bred with a Toy Poodle to create these cute creatures.
  6. Goldendoodles make great service pets.
    • The Golden Retriever's obedience and the Poodle's intelligence renders these dogs the perfect service animal.
